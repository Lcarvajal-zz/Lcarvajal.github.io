Bio

At 19, Lukas was studying pure mathematics at the Free University of Berlin. Inspired by the Berlin tech scene, he set out to earn ten thousand dollars so that he could fund his own startup.

In pursuit of this goal, Lukas built mobile apps for small startups which turned into Apps by Lukas.

After developing apps for over two years, Lukas began working on Tip Yourself which at the time only had around 100 users. Within three months he turned it into a Best New App on the Apple App Store, brought in over 30,000 downloads, and reached the 19th spot for Top Finance Apps on the Apps Store.
